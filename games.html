Хуруудах – Huruudakh (finger guessing game)

This game is like the Mongolian version of rock-paper-scissors. Two people count "one, two, three" and then stick out one finger each at the same time. The fingers have ranks:

  • Thumb beats index.
  • Index beats middle.
  • Middle beats ring.
  • Ring beats pinky.
  • Pinky beats thumb.

If both players show fingers with no rank difference, they play again. To win a point, you need to show a finger that beats your opponent's finger. You play to three points, and each player keeps track of their points on their other hand.
