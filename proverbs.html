Proverbs

"Don't judge a book by it's cover" - Царайны сайхнаар цай сүлэхгүй

English Equivalent: "A beauty cannot give you milk tea".

"If you drink the water, follow the customs" -

English Equivalent: Do as the Romans do.

"Two rabbits with one arrow" -

English Equivalent: Two birds with one stone.

"Don't cry over spilt milk" -

English Equivalent:

"If you try your best, your destiny will change" - Эзэн хичээвэл заяа хичээнэ.

English Equivalent: You get out what you put in.

"If you have good intentions, you will get yellow oil" - Сайн санааны үзүүрт шар тос

English Equivalent: Karma or if you have good intentions you will be blessed.

"Wolves come during the rain" - Чоно борооноор

English Equivalent: Bad things usually happen during hard times.

"A man fails seven times and rises eight times" - Хүн долоон удаа бүтэлгүйтэж, найман удаа босдог

English Equivalent: a person that fails will keep growing and learning from their mistakes and will eventually become better

It's easier to catch an escaped horse than to take back your word" - Өндөр барихыг хүссэн хүн гүн ухах ёстой

English Equivalent: Don’t say things carelessly as you can’t take words back.
